How Krishibot Works

Krishibot is your personal AI farming assistant, making expert agricultural advice as easy as sending a message. We've simplified the process into three quick steps, so you can get reliable solutions right when you need them.

Step 1: Ask Your Question

Got a problem in your field? Simply type or speak your question into our app in your local language. You can also take a photo of an affected plant—like a leaf with spots or a stem with wilting—and upload it. Krishibot is designed to understand what you're seeing.

Step 2: Instant Analysis

Once you send your query, Krishibot's advanced AI immediately gets to work. It compares your information or photo against a massive database of crop data, pest and disease patterns, and proven agricultural practices. This instant analysis allows us to quickly identify the issue and find the best possible solution.

Step 3: Get Your Solution

In a matter of seconds, Krishibot provides you with a clear, actionable answer. You'll get a diagnosis of the problem and a simple, step-by-step recommendation on how to treat it. Whether it's advice on a specific fertilizer, a natural remedy for pests, or a change in irrigation, Krishibot gives you the knowledge to protect your harvest and improve your yield.
